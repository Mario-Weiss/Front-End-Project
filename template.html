Full Stack Web Development

Be fully prepared (be Fullstack)
Modularily structured, bootcamp style
Combining Front- and Back-End

Course Content

Our Full Stack training is structured modularly in two parts: the front-end and the back-end. You can book them separately but best job chances you will get taking the full course. After completing our full-stack course you can apply for either front-end, back-end or full stack development positions.

Front-end - Front end is everything the user sees when he gets into interaction with a webpage or web application. On the front-end development training you learn how to build up a website, how to add elements such as text, images or videos. On top of that, you will also learn jQuery, Bootstrap and a bit of Angular. As a Front-end developer, you are able to set up stunning websites that are interactive and look awesome on every device (responsive). Front-end developers are hugely in-demand around the globe.

Read more

Back-end course - Back-end developers work on programmed solutions that run in the background of a website or web-application. This is also called server-side programming, where the majority of brainwork takes place. For example when you list used cars on a website, the system has to read out and filter the information from a database that is stored on the server. Without back-end programming, it would not be possible.
